Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our attestation services

Document attestation is the process of verifying the authenticity of documents by relevant authorities. It's required when using documents in foreign countries for purposes like employment, education, or immigration.

The attestation timeline varies depending on the document type and destination country. Typically, it takes between 7-21 working days. We also offer express services for urgent requirements.

Apostille is a specific form of attestation used for documents intended for use in countries that are part of the Hague Convention. Regular attestation is required for non-Hague Convention countries and involves verification by multiple authorities.

Yes, most attestation processes require original documents. However, some authorities may accept notarized copies. We'll guide you on the specific requirements for your documents and destination country.

We provide attestation services for all major countries including UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Oman, USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and many European and Asian countries.
